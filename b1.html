Interim Budget 2024: A trailer of the visionary roadmap for ..

TIMESOFINDIA.COM | Jul 4, 2023, 17:51 IST

The finance minister set the stage for a "Viksit Bharat by 2047", on the core principles of ‘Sabka Saath’, ‘Sabka Vikas’, ‘Sabka Vishwas’, with ‘Sabka Prayas’ being added as a new theme in the interim Budget.

This Budget's four core strategic areas came as GYAN: G for Garib Kalyan, Y for Youth empowerment, A for Anna-data i.e., farmer welfare and N for Nari utthan i.e., support for women entrepreneurs.
